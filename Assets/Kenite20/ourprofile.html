OUR PROFILE

Kenite System Solutions Profile

Be our client to benefit from our high quality products and services: –


Kenite Systems Solutions Ltd was established to provide information technology solutions for small to medium-sized businesses. Our mission from the very first day has been to cultivate professional relationships with our clients to provide effective and reliable information technology solutions for their needs.


Kenite Systems Solutions Ltd consists of a dynamic team of dedicated professionals who are committed to delivering the level of quality services and value to our customers.


As an established technology-intensive company, we pride ourselves on providing a comprehensive suite of solutions comprising of Infor SunSystems consultancy, on/offsite services, custom software & web development, Cloud data backups, Security systems (CCTV/Access control/Alarms) installations and Supply of technology equipments.


Our team consistently delivers state-of-the-art solutions in various areas including, but not limited to, integrated business solutions, system applications, product development, Internet/Intranet applications and communication & network management services.
