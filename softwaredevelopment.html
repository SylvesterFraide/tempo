SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT, IMPLEMENTATION, INTEGRATION AND MAINTENANCE


We develop customized software for small to medium enterprises to meet your company's unique needs at affordable prices. When the vision goes out of the box, packaged products can fail to meet the need of the business. Powered by 7 years in IT, kenite’s teams of QA’s, designers, engineers, and other experts are ready to analyze the idea top to bottom and make the product come true.

We draft a software requirements specification, advises clients on the development approach, and kickstart the project sticking to time and budget limits. And after our customer is happy with the result, we support the project with customization and maintenance options.


Modern business environment generates the need to use different IT solutions for different purposes to ensure the effective work of an enterprise. In this respect, system integration is a vital point to consider, whether you are planning to add new functionality to the IT solutions already in use or implement new software.

Our consultants are ready to help you find a quick and less painful way to system integration, identify milestones, and mitigate risks.
